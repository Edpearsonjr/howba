                              Transactions

                              May 3, 1967: Drafted by the San Francisco Warriors in the 6th round (63rd pick) of the 1967 NBA Draft.

                              May 11, 1970: Drafted by the Portland Trail Blazers from the San Francisco Warriors in the NBA expansion draft.

                              October 2, 1972: Traded by the Portland Trail Blazers to the Philadelphia 76ers for Dave Wohl.

                              February 1, 1973: Traded by the Philadelphia 76ers to the Atlanta Hawks for Don May. Schlueter was sent to Atlanta to complete the trade on April 18, 1973.

                              August 29, 1974: Sold by the Atlanta Hawks to the Buffalo Braves.

                              October 20, 1976: Waived by the Buffalo Braves.

                              December 8, 1976: Signed as a free agent with the Phoenix Suns.

                              July 15, 1977: Traded by the Phoenix Suns to the Portland Trail Blazers for a 1978 4th round draft pick (Wayne Smith was later selected).

                              April 24, 1978: Retired from the Portland Trail Blazers.
